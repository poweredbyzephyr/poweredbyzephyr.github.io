Our Mission

We're preventing the worst effects of climate change by removing CO2 from the atmosphere at scale.

How it's done

Our Process

Using silicate minerals and electrolysis, we generate hydrogen gas while capturing CO2. This process can operate at scale, while being economcially viable, mitigating one metric ton of CO2 for $64.

Capturing CO2

Atmospheric CO2 is pumped into Zephyr's containment chambers. Then, silicate minerals in electrolysis cells react with CO2 and convert it into bicarbonates.

Generating Clean Energy

Hydrogen gas is produced when silicate reacts with CO2 and can be used as clean energy. Unlike natural gas, hydrogen produced from electrolysis emits no CO2, and can be used as a clean fuel.

Safe Disposal of CO2

Zephyr converts CO2 bicarbonates that can be safely disposed of into the ocean for long term storage while also deacidifying the water, preserving reef and coral ecosystems.

Efficient and Valuable

Generating hydrogen gas, which can be used for fuel, allows Zephyr to mitigate 40% more CO2 than it captures, while also generating additional economic value.
